Our Mission
SSADC at YorkU, which stands for Student Support Application Development Council at YorkU, is a council led and organized by students of York University with the goal of developing applications and media for helping the student body. We provide mutual support and shared resources to all students who want to add a new project to the union, contribute to projects, or help in improving the community.
